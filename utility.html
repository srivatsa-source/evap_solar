UTILITY-SCALE SOLAR & ZINC BATTERY STORAGE

Empowering Utilities for a Reliable, Clean Grid

Utilities are at the forefront of the energy transition, tasked with decarbonizing the grid while ensuring unwavering reliability and affordability. We provide a powerful combination of high-efficiency solar generation and long-duration zinc battery energy storage systems (BESS), designed specifically for the challenges of the modern grid.

Learn More

Achieve Decarbonization and Meet Clean Energy Mandates

  • Massive Generation Capacity: Deploying solar farms allows you to add hundreds of megawatts of clean, renewable power to your generation portfolio, directly contributing to sustainability goals.
  • Predictable and Low-Cost Energy: Solar provides predictable daytime power generation with zero fuel cost, hedging against the price volatility of fossil fuels and lowering the overall cost of energy for your customers.

Ensure Grid Stability with Advanced Zinc Batteries

  • Firming Renewables for 24/7 Power: Zinc batteries store excess solar energy generated during the day and dispatch it during evening peak hours or on cloudy days. This transforms an intermittent resource into a reliable, on-demand power source.
  • Essential Grid Services: Our BESS can perform critical ancillary services, including frequency regulation, voltage support, and spinning reserve, which helps maintain grid stability and improves power quality across your network.
  • Enhanced Safety and Sustainability: Zinc battery chemistry utilizes non-flammable, water-based electrolytes, drastically reducing the risk of thermal runaway and fire associated with other battery technologies. Furthermore, zinc is an abundant, low-cost, and easily recyclable material, ensuring a more sustainable and ethical supply chain.

Why Zinc? The Superior Choice for Grid-Scale Storage

Feature Zinc Battery Systems Conventional Lithium-ion
Safety Inherently non-flammable due to aqueous electrolytes. Eliminates risk of thermal runaway. Uses flammable organic electrolytes, requiring complex and costly fire suppression systems.
Supply Chain Zinc is abundant globally, ethically sourced, and low-cost. Reliant on concentrated and volatile markets for lithium and cobalt.
Lifespan Highly durable with minimal capacity degradation, allowing for deep, consistent cycling for 10-20 years. Capacity fades over time, particularly in high-temperature environments.
Environmental Easily and highly recyclable materials, with a lower overall environmental impact. Recycling processes are complex, costly, and still developing.
