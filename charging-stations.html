India's First Sovereign EV Charging Network

Autonomous, renewable energy-powered charging stations for automobiles. A plug-and-play, subscription-based solution designed for the future.

Discover GARUDA Get Started

Revolutionary Charging Infrastructure

GARUDA is an autonomous, renewable energy-powered charging station for automobiles developed by EVAP Solar Pvt Ltd. Presented as India's first sovereign EV charging network, it's designed to be a plug-and-play, subscription-based solution.

While our long-term vision is a completely grid-free system, GARUDA initially utilizes a combination of grid and solar energy to ensure reliable charging in all conditions.

Key Advantages

Flexible Setup

Designed to be placed anywhere without needing extra space or construction. Installation completed within an hour.

Portability

Skid-mounted unit design makes it easy to move and relocate anywhere in under four hours.

Reliability

Built to offer charging reliability in all weather conditions. Valuable during power outages and for disaster management.

Rapid Deployment

Full-speed charger deployed in under a day. Suitable for locations from delivery hubs to highways.

Innovation and Technology

EVAP Solar combines technological advancements with a unique business model to maximize impact.

Integrated Design

Pre-certified, best-in-class components including Waaree Solar panels and LiFePO4 Batteries. Integrated into a rugged, portable form factor optimized for Indian conditions.

AI-Optimized Software

Proprietary AI algorithm transforms the charger into a self-healing, self-optimizing asset. Optimizes energy use between solar, battery, and grid for lowest cost per charge.

  • Predictive energy forecasting and maintenance
  • Autonomous load balancing
  • Dynamic pricing and demand management

Business Model Innovation

First of its kind in India

Energy-as-a-Service (EaaS)

EVAP Solar owns and maintains the charging stations. Customers pay a simple monthly subscription or per-use fee, eliminating upfront costs.

  • Zero capital investment required
  • Flexible pricing options
  • Complete maintenance included

Franchisee-Driven Growth

Empowers local entrepreneurs to own and operate a GARUDA unit, creating a distributed charging network across the country.

  • Local ownership opportunities
  • Distributed network expansion
  • Community-based charging solutions
